# Product Context

## Register

product

## Users
- **Target User**: Content creators and writers who want to monitor multiple content inspiration feeds, learn from classic books, trace industry trends, and study benchmark creators.
- **Context**: Used daily during research and script drafting phases. High visual hierarchy is needed to avoid information overload.
- **Workflow**: The user logs in, glances at the Dashboard metrics, clicks through tabs to browse books/models, reads news alerts, views distilled competitor formulas, and pulls copy structures.

## Product Purpose
An elegant **Information Source Monitor (信息源监控)** that serves as a creator's personal database and analytics suite. It brings together theory, benchmark analysis, news, and trending topics to streamline the content creation workflow.

## Brand Personality
- **Aesthetic**: **Editorial Magazine (杂志感排版)**. Minimalist, premium, high contrast, clean typography, generous whitespace.
- **Keywords**: Sophisticated, Intellectual, Calm, Clear.
- **Emotional Goal**: Evoke a sense of focus and professional confidence, similar to reading a high-end design or business print magazine.

## Anti-references
- **Banned**: Blue-purple neon tech dark modes, cyber glow, glassmorphism templates, typical SaaS metric card grids, small tracked uppercase subtitles on every section.
- **Why**: These are standard "AI slop" styles that feel cheap, cluttered, and generic.

## Design Principles
1. **Typographic Hierarchy First**: Style with strong contrast between serif headings and sans-serif body text. Use whitespace to group elements instead of borders.
2. **Tabbed Focus**: Dedicate each information source to its own clean page to prevent cognitive overload, with a unified central Dashboard.
3. **Data Integrity**: All feeds (including distilled competitor data) flow from a single database, enabling future automated crawler integration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **Contrast**: Maintain strict WCAG AA standards (≥ 4.5:1 contrast ratio) with charcoal text on warm paper backgrounds, or warm bone backgrounds.
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect system preferences, executing GSAP transitions gracefully with a simple crossfade fallback.
